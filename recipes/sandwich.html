How to Make Pulled Pork

Making barbecue-restaurant-worthy pulled pork is easier to make than you think.

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • (4 pound) pork shoulder roast
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup chicken barbecue sauce
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard
  • 1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 extra large onion, chopped
  • 2 large cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 8 hamburger buns, split
  • 2 tablespoons butter, or as needed

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.

  2. Pour vegetable oil into the bottom of a slow cooker. Place pork roast into the slow cooer

    3. Pour in barbecue sauce, vinegar, and chicken broth. Stir in brown sugar, yellow mustard,

    worcestershine sauce, chili powder, onion, garlic and thyme. Cover and cook on low for 10 - 12 hours

    or high for 5 to 6 hours until pork shreds easily with a fork.

  3. Remove pork from slow cooker, and shred the meat using two forks. Return shredded pork to

    slow cooker, and stir to combine with juices.

  4. Spread the inside of both halves of hamburger buns with butter. Toat buns, butter-side down

    in a skillet over medium heat until golden brown. Spoon pulled pork into toatesd buns.

  5. Serve and enjoy!
